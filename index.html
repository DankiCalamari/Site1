About Us

Birddog Music is a band located in Mildura that consists of four active members and a small team of writers. Birddog was initially created by Courtney McMullan and one of their writers Jackson. After posting cover videos online for a few months, Courtney, along with the newest member, Rhys, decided to attempt creating music. Here the two are today with drummer Xavier and bass player recording, writing and playing music live.
